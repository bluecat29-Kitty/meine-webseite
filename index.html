Making a list with points of basic framework
  • Make a construction of the website
  • Write the important content of the website
  • Make a structure of the content: How should it looks like?
  • Don't forget the footer-function.
  • Check everything again with validator.
  • Use the css-function to color and style the website.
  • Check everything again with validator.

Minimum-content of the website

  1. Put a head-function and give every head-function a title.
  2. Don't use too much head-functions.
  3. Color the the heads; but also not too much.
  4. Use the table-function to summarize some points with explanations.
  5. Style the table.
  6. Use background image or background-color.
  7. Don't forget the forms.
  8. Put links.
  9. Use pictures or media-field to show something.
  10. And also important is to use the nav-function.
